Projects

Project-1:

Title: Hyper Spectral Image Classification

  • “Hyper-spectral image classification” with 3DCNNs involves the integration of spectral and spatial information in hyper-spectral imagery using three-dimensional convolutional neural networks.
  • This approach finds applications in areas like remote sensing, land cover analysis, and environmental monitoring, enabling precise object and land cover classification..
  • Technologies used: Python, Jupyter Notebook, HTML, and CSS.

Project-2:

Title: Online Shopping Cart (E-Commerce Website)

  • This Online Shopping Cart project allows users to register, browse, search, and filter products, add items to their cart, and proceed with a demo payment.
  • Users can manage their orders and receive email notifications for order confirmation, shipping status, and stock updates. Admins handle inventory management, product updates, and order tracking.
  • Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Oracle database.
